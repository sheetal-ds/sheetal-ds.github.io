Description: Businesses often strive to understand what people talk about their products and ways to improve. In this project, I extracted amazon reviews on top 5 laptop brands to understand customer sentiments and provide specific actionable insights into improving audience experience / expectations.

Using word2vec embeddings, I converted text to vector form, such that each word could be represented in numerical vector format. A review containing multiple such words was then converted to vector format by averaging the vectors of the words it contained.

Sentiment of a review was identified using supervised machine learning classification techniques like Recurrent Neural Networks and Logistic Regression. Dominant topic of each rveiew was identified by performing LDA Topic Modelling technique.

Once I had the sentiment and topic of each review, I identified charatcteristics of laptops that customers are happy about (battery life, display) and charatcteristics that customers complained about (quality & performance, service).
