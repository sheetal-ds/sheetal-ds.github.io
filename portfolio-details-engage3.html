Project Information

  • Category: Machine Learning
  • Project date: 17 Jun, 2023
  • Models Used: Multi-Criteria Decision Making Model (TOPSIS), Logistic Regression
  • Tools & Libraries : Python, Snowflake, SQL, Pandas, Numpy, Scipy

Description: Engage3 measures how consumers view retailers' prices and helps retailers optimize the prices they offer, curated for thousands of items, hundreds of locations and millions of customers.

Proprietary Engage3 pricing model gave outputs such as Freeze the Price (No price change) or Change the price up by 20% , etc. There was a need of easy-to-understand metric, akin to a credit score, which would consolidate forecasting into customer-friendly scorecard rating

I explored logistic regression, other classification techniques to perform this. The best method to achieve the validation score goal is by using a multi criteria decision making model - TOPSIS (Technique for Order of Preference by Similarity to Ideal Solution)

Input to the model were error terms of the price forecast and other measures like number of data points used for prediction, price standard deviation, etc. And output was a validation score. By looking at this, customers of Engage3 would achieve more confident decision making (High score implies the retailer should incorporate the suggestion while low score suggests vice versa)
