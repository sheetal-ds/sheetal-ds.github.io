Description: The objective of this project was to investigate how the value of industries and stock prices changed during COVID, and how these trends can be compared to other market/societal measures. Showcase the story through impactful visualization

Steps include - Visualizing the market cap of various indices from 2017â€“present (i.e. DOW, S&P, Russel), Selecting individual stocks of business models impacted during COVID, Compare the valuations of these industries post-COVID to their value pre-COVID and, Relate the valuation of high, low, or regularly performing industries.

Data Sources include Google Finance, Data Hub, Kaggle, The Databank of the World bank, UN Data, GBD Results. Industries Analyzed: Airlines, Entertainment and Pharma

Key takeaways: Unprecedented spike in trade volume in key industries, Online entertainment industry spiked during COVID but the effects were not consistent in long run, The Health industry maintained its positive momentum
